Superior Bio - Conservancy

An ambitious plan to protect and restore the biological integrity of the Laurentian Forest Province and the Lake Superior Watershed. The work that I have been doing here is mostly cartography and land ethic mapping. However, in recent months we have multiple interns working as a team to put together a corridor design to connect fragmented pieces of public land. You can learn more about the work we do at SBC here!
